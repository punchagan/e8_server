RULE BOOK



BONUS CRITERION- To win a bonus point, the chasing team should complete the target within 6 overs or the team fielding second should Bowl-out the opposite Team within 75% of their total score.


  1. Teams should assemble in the ground 10 minutes prior to the start of the play.

  2. Toss will be taken 5 minutes before the start of the play. If any team is not assembled before the toss then that team will lose the eligibility of a Bonus point.

  3. Except the Batsmen all other players of the batting side should sit in the pavilion.

  4. Umpires decision is final and binding. No arguments with the Umpire should be made by either of the Team players. In extreme situations the Batsmen or the Bowling Team Captain can take his stand. If any other person is involved then a strict action will be taken against that Team.

  5. Only Batsmen are eligible to appeal for a Fast bowl. Its umpire’s decision to term the ball as fast or not after the Batsmen appeal.

  6. If the ball is ruled as fast by the umpire then the ball is considered as dead.

  7. The whole body of the Wicket-keeper should be behind the wicket until the ball reaches the bat. Otherwise it is considered as a No-ball.

  8. No-ball has a penalty of 1-run.

  9. A free-hit is awarded to the batsman for a No-Ball bowled. (Only valid on leg-no-balls).

  10. Two wides are allowed per over. If this condition is exceeded then one run penalty is given for every extra wide thereon.

  11. Bowler should specify his guard [Over or around], failing which the ball can be declared as a No-ball.

  12. Runs cannot be scored behind the wicket. [even if it comes out after going back]


  1. The Team winning the match will be awarded 2 points and in case the team wins with a Bonus then an extra point will be awarded.


  1. DRESS CODE- All Teams should come dressed with their prescribed Uniform. No shoes of any kind are allowed. Only a player having an injury in his leg can wear shoes with the permission of the Umpire. Umpire’s decision will be final and binding.

  2. Next Batsman should be in the field within 1 minute from the time when the previous Batsman is dismissed, failing which the Batsman will be declared out.

  3. Underarm Bowling is Not allowed.

  4. Ball hit directly to the side walls by the Batsmen is considered to be out. [Except when hit to the quadrangle wall.]

  5. If the ball is hit outside the arena then the batsman is given out. (Not-out if the ball lands on the roof at the Bowling end).

  6. The ball which lands and skids into the batsman (LOW-BALL only) may be ruled dead, the decision of which lies at the Umpire’s discretion.

  7. If the ball pitches the second time after it crosses the batsman then it is considered to be CORRECT BALL.(Only two pitches are allowed)

  8. A stumping by the keeper will be considered only if he collects the ball before it hits any of the walls.

  9. In case of a serious misbehavior with the Umpire or the opposite team, the player and the captain will be warned for the first time and thereafter if it continues penalty will be enforced on the concerned team(+5 runs or 3-ball exemption whichever is applicable…)
